Privacy Policy for FontScope

Effective Date: November 1, 2025

Introduction

FontScope ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

FontScope does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The extension operates entirely locally on your device and only reads CSS properties of web pages you visit to display font information.

How We Use Information

Since we don't collect any information, there is no usage of personal data. The extension simply provides font identification functionality through local computation.

Data Security

Your privacy is paramount. FontScope doesn't store or share any data, so there are no security risks related to data transmission or storage.

Third-Party Services

FontScope does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs.
